# Diana Optima

Welcome to the official page of **Diana Optima** — a personal content publishing and automation tool.

## About

Diana Optima is a lightweight tool that helps creators prepare, organize, and upload their own video content to social media platforms. It streamlines the routine steps of content publishing so creators can focus on making videos.

## What It Does

* Receives video content submitted by the account owner
* Processes and prepares videos for publishing (formatting, metadata)
* Uploads content to the owner's connected social media accounts via official platform APIs
* Notifies the owner about the publishing status

## How It Works

Diana Optima connects to social media accounts exclusively through official OAuth authorization. The tool only publishes content explicitly submitted by the account owner and never posts on behalf of third parties.
